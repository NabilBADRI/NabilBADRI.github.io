Publications

"My publications revolve around the pervasive issue of inappropriate content on internet platforms. Despite established regulations, there's a growing disclosure of offensive content. My work focuses on leveraging recent advancements in artificial intelligence, particularly deep learning, to automatically detect and categorize such content. This approach not only addresses the immediate challenge but also establishes connections with personality analysis, paving the way for tasks like identifying aggressive personalities, sentiment analysis, detecting stress, and uncovering fake accounts."

Article 1

Badri, N., Kboubi, F., & Chaibi, A. H. (2022). Combining FastText and Glove word embedding for offensive and hate speech text detection. Procedia Computer Science, 207, 769-778.

Article 2

Badri, N., Kboubi, F., & Habacha Chaibi, A. (2022, September). Towards Automatic Detection of Inappropriate Content in Multi-dialectic Arabic Text. In International Conference on Computational Collective Intelligence (pp. 84-100). Cham: Springer International Publishing.
